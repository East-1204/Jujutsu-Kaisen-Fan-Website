About the Series

Jujutsu Kaisen follows the story of Yuji Itadori, an ordinary high school student who becomes entangled in the world of Jujutsu Sorcery after swallowing a powerful cursed object - the finger of Ryomen Sukuna, the King of Curses.

Now a student at Tokyo Jujutsu High, Yuji must learn to harness cursed energy while collecting all of Sukuna's fingers to save humanity.

Main Characters

Yuji Itadori

The main protagonist who becomes the vessel of Sukuna. Despite his extraordinary situation, Yuji maintains his strong moral compass and dedicates himself to helping others.

Megumi Fushiguro

A stoic and powerful sorcerer who uses the Ten Shadows Technique. Though initially cold, he forms a strong bond with Yuji and fights to protect the weak.

Nobara Kugisaki

A fierce and confident sorcerer from the countryside. She uses her Straw Doll Technique to fight cursed spirits and never backs down from a challenge.

Satoru Gojo

The strongest jujutsu sorcerer in the world. Despite his immense power and playful personality, he takes his role as a teacher seriously.

Ryomen Sukuna

Is one of the main antagonists in the anime and manga series Jujutsu Kaisen. He is a legendary curse spirit regarded as the "King of Curses" due to his immense power and malevolence.

Yuta Okkotsu

Initially a timid and introverted high school student, Yuta becomes a powerful jujutsu sorcerer after being haunted by the cursed spirit of his childhood friend, Rika Orimoto.
